Tandoori Paneer Tikka

Description

Cubes of paneer, onion and bell pepper are marinated in a flavorful yogurt marinade and then either grilled or roasted in an oven to perfection!

Paneer tikka is a popular Indian Appetizer with a super flavorful marinade and is usually cooked in a traditional clay oven.

This version prepared in an oven makes it so easy to make, but tastes just as good!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp of ginger + garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil of choice
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 3/4 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp dried fenugreek
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp cilantro
  • 2 tbsp mint
  • 2 cups of paneer, cubed
  • 1 large onion, cubed
  • 1 large bell pepper, cubed - the more colorful the better!
  • Cilantro-mint chutney, to serve

Steps

  1. Whisk the yogurt till smooth and add ginger+garlic paste, oil, coriander powder, chaat masala, red chili powder, garam masala, dried fenugreek, salt, lemon juice and the chopped herbs. Whisk to combine.
  2. Stir in the cubed paneer, onions and bell peppers until coated and then cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hr but ideally overnight
  3. After marinating, arrange the cubes on skewers while preheating the oven to 450F
  4. Line a baking tray with parchment paper and place the skewers and spray with cooking spray to avoid them drying out
  5. Bake the tikka for 10 minutes and then broil for 2-3 minutes till charred
  6. Remove the tikka from the oven and let cool before serving with cilantro-mint chutney and enjoy!
